Base condition
The proposed SLA
The business offers one service metric: time-on-site under target. Reasonable on paper. The drawing will show it is the wrong number to sign.
Rev 01 · the conventional path
A traditional IT firm
Takes the metric at face value. Route optimizer, faster mobile app, a tighter visit. The trip gets shorter. It still repeats. They hit the SLA and miss the outcome.
Rev 02 · reads behavior
Researcher
Shadows the crew. Sees the technician arrive without the part. The insight: they are not slow, they are coming back.
Rev 03 · proves or kills
Data Engineer
Wires the systems together and tests the claim. Time-on-site gives way to first-time-fix, the metric that actually carries the SLA.
Rev 04 · feasibility
AI Engineer
Marks where AI moves the proven signal. Eliminate the return trip. Never a model the signal has not earned.
The through-line
